Hello!

The name's Yuki Heather Morgan. I'm a writer, both creative and non, currently located in the US state of Massachusetts. I like to think my background in English Literature and Environmental Policy and Decision Making—shout-out to my beloved alma mater, the University of Puget Sound!—comes through in my work. Death, regeneration, humanity, and monstrosity are themes I instinctively gravitate to. The network of connections between them never ends.

Embracing my queerness and biracial identity (Japanese and Welsh) is deeply important to me. Representation matters, and sustainability isn't sustainable if it isn't also equitable. Creative expression connects people together; legislation, protest, and especially donations materially benefit marginalized groups. Sites like Great Nonprofits can help you find local causes to support.
